Welcome to my PORTFOLIO

Be kind to yourself. Kind enough. Plant a tree. Watch it grow. Write a song. Paint a picture. Write a poem, a song. Write letters. Write birthday wishes to friends and family. Create. Move. Or stay still.
Make mistakes, small and big. Take decisions, good and bad.
Breathe.
Breathe.
Learn. Unlearn. Grow.
Touch and stroke animals. Hug people a little longer. Keep your hands warm.
Have a little faith. And lots of hope.
And be kind.
Save money. Save enough to buy that original album.
Buy it and listen to it on Wednesday nights. Share your playlist.
Laugh shamelessly. Cry honestly. And please, be kind. Be very kind.
